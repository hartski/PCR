Hi - We're Pearl's Cab Ride

about

Pearl's Cab Ride - what sort of a name is that?????!  

After 25 years, no-one around these parts really asks that question anymore. These three words are more than a name - Pearl's Cab Ride is a group of like-minded players, who carry the torch for everything that is still good in LIVE funk and soul music.

The band’s roots took hold in the Acid Jazz scene of the early 1990’s, (yes, such a musical movement did make it to the edges of the East Yorkshire coast!), when founder member Lyn Acton and original guitarist Karl Arthur bonded over their love of all things soulful and funky, and the rest as they say, is funk-history.

The band’s musical roots hold fast today, however they are not a retro outfit, and are still writing and performing new music in their sweat soaked live performances... These people are in the pocket, and very soon you will be too!
